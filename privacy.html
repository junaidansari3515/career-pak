Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. This page explains exactly how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

Career Pakistan may collect information you provide directly (such as contact form details), technical data (browser, device type, IP, pages visited), and preference data (saved items, dark mode, or browsing behavior) to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver scholarships, jobs, internships, exams, and study resources efficiently.
  • To respond to inquiries, feedback, and support requests.
  • To improve website performance, quality, and user experience.
  • To monitor platform safety and prevent abuse.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and local storage to remember preferences, maintain essential functionality, and understand traffic patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools for analytics, hosting, or functionality. These services may process limited technical data under their own privacy terms. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Information may only be shared when required by law, to protect users and platform integrity, or with service providers who support website operations under confidentiality obligations.

6. Data Security

We apply practical safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, or alteration. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request correction or deletion of information you submitted via forms.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications.
  • You may control cookies through browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Career Pakistan is intended for students and professionals. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data from children without appropriate consent.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy as our platform evolves. Updated versions are posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related concerns, please contact us through the Contact page.
